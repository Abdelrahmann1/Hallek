Hallek Production — Privacy Policy

Media production agency in Saudi Arabia. We create ads and content for brands.

Last updated: 2025/26/9

Introduction

Hallek Production respects user privacy. We explain what we collect and why. We follow Saudi law and platform rules.

Data we collect

  • Contact data: name, email, phone, company when you submit a form.
  • Device and usage data: IP, browser, page visits, ad interaction, referrer.
  • Content you share: messages, media files you upload by consent.

How we use data

  • To respond to inquiries and deliver services.
  • To run and measure ads on social platforms and search engines.
  • To improve site performance and user experience.
  • To send marketing only with your consent. You can opt out anytime.

Sharing and third parties

We do not sell personal data.

We share data only with service providers that need it to operate services. Examples: hosting, analytics, ad platforms, and payment providers.

Cookies and tracking

We use cookies and similar tools to track visits and ad performance. You can block cookies in your browser. Blocking may limit features.

Your rights

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion.
  • Opt out of marketing messages.

To exercise rights contact us below. We respond within a reasonable time.

Data security

We use technical and organizational measures to protect data. No internet system is 100% secure.

Legal requests

We may disclose data to comply with laws or to protect rights.
